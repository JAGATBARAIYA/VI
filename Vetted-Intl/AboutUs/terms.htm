Comprehensive service availability for the Traveler’s Registered Trip is available with full adherence to terms and conditions. Pre-trip travel assistance is available from the date of subscription thru the end of the trip. Services are terminated at midnight on the last day of the declared trip or when the Traveler reaches their originating destination (whichever comes first)

1.11FREQUENT TRAVELER is a Member taking multiple trips during a 12 month period where no single trip exceeds 45 days in length and which is more than 100 miles away from the Member’s location of permanent residence
